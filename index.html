About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, working with Prof. Aristides Gionis.

I am a researcher with a deep interest in Network Science, Graph Algorithms, and Graph Machine Learning. I completed my PhD at ETH Zürich, where I worked under the guidance of Prof. Ulrik Brandes. My research focused on Graph Algorithms and machine learning with graphs.

Prior to ETH, I earned my Master degree's from Purdue University and IIIT Hyderabad, specializing in graph algorithms and high-performance computing. My academic journey is deeply rooted in designing efficient algorithms for large-scale graph problems and applying ML techniques to unstructured data.

I have also worked at Nvidia as a Senior Software Engineer, contributing to embedded systems for automotive use. Outside of research, I enjoy hiking and exploring nature.
